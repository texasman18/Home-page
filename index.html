How to Use

Using YAM

1

Open the link

Tap the button above or visit the YAM URL.

2

Allow location

Grant access and the map appears automatically.

3

See nearby restrooms

Pins appear on the map showing the closest options.

4

Get directions

Tap a pin for distance info and navigation.
