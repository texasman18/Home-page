What is YAM?

The fastest way to find a public restroom near you —
real-time and location-based.

YAM

Your Area Map · Restroom Finder

When every second counts,
find the nearest restroom in 1 second

YAM uses your current GPS location to instantly display nearby public restrooms on a map via the Kakao Maps API. No app installation required — just open the link and go. Add it to your home screen for app-like speed.

How to Use

It's this
simple

1

Open the Link

Visit the YAM URL in your browser or tap the button above.

2

Allow Location

Grant location access and the map appears automatically.

3

See Nearby Restrooms

Pins appear on the map showing restrooms closest to you.

4

Get Directions

Tap a pin to see distance info and navigate there instantly.
